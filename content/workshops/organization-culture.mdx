## Introduction


- How constructive is your organization’s culture?
- How inclusive is it?
- To what degree do politics and control issues adversely impact performance? 
- How do customers perceive your organization, and how are they impacted by the behaviors of your employees?


##  Organization Culture Workshop
This Workshop is focused on the attributes of a carefully designed organization culture.


<Steps>

  <Step>
  Culture Profile
  </Step>
You will begin by completing a culture profile to determine how constructive or counter-productive
  your organization’s culture is.

  <Step>
  Identify
  </Step>
 You will identify the different attributes that affect employee 
  engagement, new employee attraction, retention, and performance.

    <Step>
  Assess
  </Step>
you will be able
   to assess areas of opportunity.

    <Step>
  Design
  </Step>
You will learn how to design, re-design, calibrate, re-calibrate,
    and determine ways that continuously improve your organization’s culture and its resulting 
    influences.

      <Step>
  Styles
  </Step>  
  See how styles of compassion can be engaged to improve desired strategies and 
    drive the change you need through the lens of culture.


</Steps>



When two organizations transition into a single culture the incongruities and differences between the two can be problematic. Similarly, the differences between two subgroups within an organization can adversely impact the performance of the organization as a whole. To facilitate alignment between the entities, you will learn how to identify shared values and behaviors and guide the respective leaders/supervisors by using carefully designed instrumentation and processes of compassion to a shared ideal
