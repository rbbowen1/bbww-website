##  Social Responsibility Workshop
This workshop provides information on the value of Stewardship. You will learn:


<Steps>

  <Step>
Thinking outside of the box
  </Step>
  what other organizations have done to harness the concept of “thinking outside of the box” by going beyond personal self-interest by embracing the concept of universal enlightenment.

  <Step>
  Analyze 
  </Step>
We will analyze the potential for stewardship in your organization.

  <Step>
  Value and potential
  </Step>
  The value and potential of each initiative will be captured, as we provide a comprehensive program of implementation.
  
  <Step>
  Return on investment
  </Step>

Estimate the return on investment, as you change the world of your leaders, your employees, your customers, and the world in which you conduct business.


  <Step>
  Legacy
  </Step>
Learn how to create a “legacy” for your organizationa and how to optimize the “legacy” of each person who is trying to reach his or her full potential.



    <Step>
  Purpose 
  </Step>

  Understand the "meaning of" and "attraction to" a shared purpose that the compassion of stewardship has for the generational differences in your organization.

    <Step>
  Seven Attributes of Stewardship
  </Step>
Explore the Seven Attributes of Stewardship:

- Values-centeredness
- Purposefulness
- Accountability
- Being Integral
- Disciplined
- Courageous



<Step>
   Major strategy action areas
  </Step>
Formulate the major strategy action areas, critical initiatives, and timelines that ensure Success.




</Steps>



Make no mistake, this workshop requires critical thinking, vision, and determination, but you will walk away with a detailed plan for building a Legacy of Compassion and Lasting Value.
