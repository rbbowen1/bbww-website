## Introduction

- What is Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)?
- Why is it important?
- What is the value of “service above self”? 
- Is your organization too busy to think outside of the box?
- Is it the case that it doesn’t want to expend the effort of time and money on what it might consider frivolous activity?


##  Social Responsibility Workshop
In this workshop you will learn:


<Steps>

  <Step>
  How to benefit from Socially Responsible
  </Step>
  how many organizations are benefiting from being Socially Responsible.

  <Step>
  Attract workforce segments
  </Step>
You will learn how appeal and attract certain segments of the workforce…and the marketplace.

  <Step>
  Engagement and market reputation
  </Step>
We will provide examples of how some organizations are benefiting by improving employee engagement and market reputation.

  <Step>
  Stand out
  </Step>

You will learn how they “stand out” as exemplary citizens, compassionate organizations, making a difference in the world.


</Steps>



Explore the metrics to lowering turnover, improving employee engagement, and increasing market reputation and awareness with the strategy of CSR. Once you identify the areas that are potential gold mines for your organization, we will work with you to formulate a CSR initiative, mapping out a detailed plan with a step-by-step time and action calendar toward  achieving your corporate objectives.
